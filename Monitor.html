How this works

From snapshot to signal

Each indicator shows two windows side by side — a five-year arc for the structural story and a one-month close-up for the current move. A scheduled job is intended to pull each latest figure from a market data feed daily and append it, so the curves build a genuine history rather than showing a single moment.

The selection is deliberate: the EU-ETS carbon price is the cost Europe puts on emitting; copper, the platinum group metals, and lithium are the physical bottlenecks of electrification, clean hydrogen, and batteries; and Brent is the fossil benchmark the whole transition is racing to undercut. Lithium is the one soft spot — no clean free real-time feed exists, so it shows a daily China-derived proxy with that caveat noted on the card.
